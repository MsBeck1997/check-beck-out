Content Strategy

Header will contain two links: one to my GitHub and another to my LinkedIn.
Jumbotron will contain pictures from my portfolio will small descriptions. They will link to the portfolio section.
Circle Sections will contain links to 'about', 'portfolio', and 'resume' sections that will open upon click.
About Section will contain a brief paragraph of who I am, and what I do as well as a professional picture.
Portfolio will contain images of the projects I've worked on with brief descriptions of them. I will inclide links to the sites.
Resume will contain my skills, what I'm proficient in, and what accomplishments I have.
Quotes will contain a few quotes from people recommending me. It will scroll through a few different ones.
Contact Form will be a form to send me an email to request more or contact me.
